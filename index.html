EVERYTHING A BUSINESS ULTRABOOK™ SHOULD BE

The EliteBook Folio 1040 was designed for professional users who require a reliable and powerful notebook in everyday work life. This outstanding notebook has a 14” LED display with HD resolution. Its casing has been CNC­milled from anodised aluminium, coupled with an all­magnesium undercarriage. Thanks to this well­conceived choice of materials, the notebook is very slim at only 1.59 cm and weighs merely 1.5 kg. The comprehensive range of functionality is enhanced by features like an integrated smart card reading device and an illuminated keyboard.

  • Windows 7 Professional 64 (available through downgrade rights
    from Windows 8.1 Pro)
  • Intel® Core™ i7­4600U with Intel HD Graphics 4400 (2.1 GHz,
    4 MB cache, 2 cores)
  • 14" (35.56 cm)
  • 8 GB 1600 MHz DDR3L SDRAM (4 GB (onboard) and 4 GB (1 x 4 GB))
  • 256 GB SATA SSD
